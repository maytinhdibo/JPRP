VNU Journal of Science: Policy and Management Studies of Vietnam National University is a scientific forum in which national and international scientists, leadership can announce their latest research achievements and exchange opinion, information about scientific issues related to the field of policy and management. The aims of the journal are to create a link between training and research; to contribute to the training of high-quality human resources; transfer of knowledge and technology; close cooperation between basic academic research and applied research; and the development of practical policy for the purpose of serving the process of industrialization, modernization and international integration in our country.
Keyword: Policy, Management, Science, Research
